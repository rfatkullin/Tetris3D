Hot keys

P Pause
F2 New Game
F3 Full screen
F4 Ambient music
F5 Save game
F6 Load game

Figures shifts

A Shift figure to the left relative to the player
D Shift figure to the right relative to the playere
W Shift figure from player to back
S Shift figure to player

Figures rotates

Rotate figure to the left relative to the player Press right button of the mouse and move it to the left and release button
Rotate figure to the right relative to the player Press right button of the mouse and move it to the right and release button
Rotate figure from player to back Press right button of the mouse and move it up and release button
Rotate figure to player Press right button of the mouse and move it down and release button
Rotate figure by clockwise in plane which parallel floor of game field Scroll down
Rotate figure by anti-clockwise in plane which parallel floor of game field Scroll up
